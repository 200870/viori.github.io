Marketing

Communication Message and Objectives

This is where you define what the communications are supposed to achieve.

Reach Objective

“To reach 100 followers on either Instagram and TikTok by 10th of April 2022.”

Affect Objective

Affect objectives - what do you want your target audience to feel?

Response Objective

“To get 250 Instagram user swipe up on Instagram Story paid advertisement to visit our website by 10th of April 2022.”

Target Audience

Females from the generation-z aged 18-24 and living in the Netherlands.

Which platforms/social media do they use?

Instagram and TikTok

How much do they use it?

Frequently. Several times a day.

What is their activity on social media platforms?

Watching Instagram stories mostly and reels sometimes. Scrolling through “for you” page on TikTok.

At what times does your target audience visit social media platforms/websites?

Mostly in the morning and in the evening

What other brands do they like?

Calvin Klein, Tommy Hilfiger, Zara, Marc Jacobs, Rituals, Victoria’s Secret

How should you position your message?

Text

What do you want to communicate?

Viori offers affordable good quality perfume that last all day.

What is their online and offline behaviour when it comes to media?

Social media is their most frequent source of media (almost the only). They use it for either entertainment as well as a source of information.

Media Tactics

We will use two different social media platforms: Instagram and TikTok.

These two platforms are most common to use by our target group. These two platforms clearly dominate the others, that is why we decided to put our focus on them and only them.

Achieving objectives:

We will record a video campaign with young Dutch females for either Instagram and Tiktok. It will consist of one main video presenting shortcuts and several videos presenting each person separately. They will be explaining what being authentic and true to themselves means for them.

Instagram

We will post stories including questionnaires and polls on stories to get an interaction from the followers. The add on stories will contain our main video campaigns or special graphic that include our message and call to action. The main video will be posted on reels, as well as the videos presenting Instagram each person separately.

TikTok

On Tik Tok we would like to create brand awareness by creating videos with our product. In the short video we would present product’s uniqueness (how they create their own scents) in order to create the viewer’s curiosity. We will post the main video and videos presenting people separately.
