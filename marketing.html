Media Tactics

In media tactics, you will - based on your target group - justify which social media platforms you will use to promote your brand with the goal to create awareness for your website. Which platforms will you use and why? How many will you use?

Explain in detail how each platform will help you achieve your objectives. Think about the unique strengths of each platform, how would you best utilize this?

Planning

Create a planning (content calendar). In this planning, you will provide an overview when and what you will communicate on which platform.

Learning Points

What went as expected and what didn’t? What would you do differently? Reflect on the past work and describe what you learned.
